ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Sam Cheng, a graphic designer with a passion for visual storytelling. Over the years, I've created everything from web layouts to illustrations. My journey began with a fascination for the intersection of art and technology, and I've honed my skills to build a successful career.

When I’m not working on projects, you’ll find me exploring new creative techniques, sketching ideas, or seeking inspiration from the world around me. I believe great design is about more than aesthetics—it’s about creating something meaningful.
